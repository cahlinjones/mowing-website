How Yarbo Handles Winter

Set it up once, enjoy clear driveways all season

1

Weather Monitoring

Yarbo connects to weather forecasts and monitors for incoming snow. You can enable auto-start or manual scheduling.

2

Automatic Activation

When snow is detected, Yarbo leaves its dock and begins clearing your mapped zones using optimal patterns.

3

Continuous Operation

As snow accumulates, Yarbo automatically returns to recharge and resumes work, clearing 24/7 until finished.

4

Monitor Remotely

Check progress via the app anytime. Watch live camera feeds, adjust settings, or manually control if needed.
